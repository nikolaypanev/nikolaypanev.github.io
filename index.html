Who are we?

We are a leading labor supply company in Bulgaria and Europe, specializing in connecting businesses across the continent with skilled and reliable workers from Asia and Africa. We streamline the recruitment process, ensuring a perfect match for your staffing needs and supporting both employers and employees throughout their journey.

At KAVHAN, we specialize in the seamless and efficient supply of skilled workers from Asia and Africa to meet the diverse needs of European businesses. Our unwavering commitment to professionalism underpins every step of our process, from rigorous talent acquisition and thorough vetting to expert visa processing and smooth onboarding. We leverage a robust international network and cutting-edge recruitment strategies to ensure a rapid and precise match for your staffing requirements, empowering your operations with exceptional global talent, professionally and efficiently.

With more than fifteen years of experience, no matter your industry or specific skill requirement, KAVHAN is your comprehensive partner. Contact us today to discuss your unique staffing needs, and let us efficiently connect you with the skilled workers.
